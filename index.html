Simulation Controls

Space: Pause/Resume

R: Reset Simulation

1-4: Load Patterns

Camera Controls

WASD / Arrows: Rotate

T: Toggle Auto-rotate

Left Mouse: Drag to Rotate

Home / Numpad-5: Reset View

Panning & Zoom

IJKL / Numpad: Pan

Q/E: Zoom In/Out
